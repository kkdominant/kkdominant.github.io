f(x)f\left( x\right)f(x)在[0,a]\left\lbrack {0,a}\right\rbrack[0,a]上可导(a>0),f(0)=1,f(a)=0\left( {a > 0}\right) ,f\left( 0\right) = 1,f\left( a\right) = 0(a>0),f(0)=1,f(a)=0,令g(x)=f(x)−xag(x)=f(x)-\dfrac{x}{a}g(x)=f(x)−ax​,则g(0)>0,g(a)<0g(0)>0,g(a)<0g(0)>0,g(a)<0,则由介值定理知存在点c∈(0,a)c \in(0,a)c∈(0,a)使得,g(c)=f(c)−ca=0g(c)=f(c)-\dfrac{c}{a} =0g(c)=f(c)−ac​=0,在(0,ca),(ca,1)(0,\dfrac{c}{a}),(\dfrac{c}{a},1)(0,ac​),(ac​,1)上分别运用Lagrange中值定理可得:
